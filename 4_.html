Introduction of the protagonist

  • SpongeBob SquarePants

    SpongeBob SquarePants is the titular character and one of the main protagonists of SpongeBob SquarePants. He is a yellow, square-shaped sponge who typically wears brown shorts, a white shirt, a red tie, and black shoes. He lives in a pineapple house in the fictional underwater city of Bikini Bottom and works as a fry cook at the famous fast-food restaurant, the Krusty Krab, where he is Mr. Krabs' best employee. He has a pet snail named Gary, and his best friend is Patrick Star.


    His hobbies include jellyfishing, blowing bubbles, and practicing karate.

  • Patrick Star

    Patrick is a pink starfish, SpongeBob’s best friend, and one of Squidward's least favorite neighbors. He wears purple floral swim trunks and is often involved in humorous antics with SpongeBob. He is unemployed but is a loyal customer of the Krusty Krab. Occasionally, he works at the Krusty Krab or the Chum Bucket, but only as a temporary employee.


    His hobbies include daydreaming, taking long naps, and eating ice cream.

  • Gary the Snail

    His full name is Garold "Gary" Wilson Snail, Jr., commonly known as Gary the Snail. He is SpongeBob’s pet snail, capable of understanding human speech and communicating in his own way. He usually stays at SpongeBob’s house and is highly intelligent. His movement leaves a trail of slime wherever he goes.


    According to a royal family genealogy, he is actually Patrick Star’s cousin.

  • Mr. Krabs

    Mr. Krabs is a red crab whose real name is Eugene H. Krabs (his nickname "Eugene" comes from his middle name). He once served in the navy and was known as "Armor Abs" during his service. He is extremely greedy and values money above all else—even his own life. He is notoriously stingy, even towards his daughter, but occasionally treats SpongeBob to a Krabby Patty. He has a daughter named Pearl, a sperm whale. His grandfather, Redbeard, was a pirate. Additionally, he once went on a date with Mrs. Puff.


    Due to his obsession with money and stinginess, he often plays an anti-hero role in many episodes.

  • Plankton

    Plankton is a tiny green copepod with only one eye. He is devious and cunning and is the arch-nemesis of Mr. Krabs.


    Due to his evil nature, he is often mistreated and looked down upon by others.

  • Squidward Tentacles

    Squidward is a large-nosed octopus belonging to the Octopus vulgaris species. He has a short temper, is snobbish, and considers himself an artist, though his talents are questionable. He is currently bald but once had long, curly yellow hair and wishes for it to grow back. He lives in a house resembling an Easter Island Moai statue, situated between SpongeBob and Patrick’s homes in Bikini Bottom.


    He is skilled at cooking.
