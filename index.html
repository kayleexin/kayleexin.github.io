SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants is a character from the American animated series SpongeBob SquarePants and its spin-offs. He lives in a city at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean called Bikini Bottom and works as a senior cook at the Krusty Krab. He is a yellow rectangular sponge with a body structure resembling a cleaning sponge. He has two arms, each with four fingers. His main attire consists of brown shorts, a white shirt, and a red tie. His body can regenerate if damaged. Although he normally eats food, he can also survive by simply filtering seawater. He is physically weak, and what he calls dumbbells are actually plush toys. However, as the story progresses, it seems that his strength gradually improves. Interestingly, when he sparred with Sandy in karate, his physical weakness was not apparent, and the two fought evenly. His common catchphrase is: "I'm ready!"
