Introduction

The story revolves around the mysterious recipe of the Krabby Patty, leading to a new round of conflict between the scheming Plankton and Mr. Krabs. Although SpongeBob successfully thwarted Plankton's aggressive attack, he still falls victim to Plankton's cunning trick. At a crucial moment, the recipe mysteriously disappears, with the immediate consequence being that no one can make the Krabby Patty anymore. For the residents of Bikini Bottom, who are heavily reliant on the Krabby Patty in their daily lives, this news is nothing short of a thunderclap. The town's peace is shattered, and the underwater world plunges into a chaotic and apocalyptic state. Due to rescuing Plankton, SpongeBob is treated as a traitor by everyone. In this moment of crisis, SpongeBob, Patrick, Plankton, Mr. Krabs, Sandy, and Squidward are able to travel through time and space, following the scent of the Krabby Patty to a crowded beach. There, they encounter the bearded pirate who stole the secret recipe using trickery, and thus, a battle to defend the Krabby Patty recipe begins.
